Origin Story

From the first church musical to the first children's theatre audition to the first professional job, theatre has been the one constant that has given me more joy than anything else in my life (even though The Wizard of Oz comes close). I love the physical spaces, the people, the culture, the creativity, the innovation, the we've-gotta-figure-this-out-quick moments, and, most of all, the community. I love it all.

Live performance is transformative and whether I'm teaching students their vocal parts, brainstorming next season's new merch ideas, or diversifying our revenue sources and balancing the budget, I always lead with integrity and artistic excellence.

My commitment to being the most well-rounded leader is what sets me apart from the rest and I intend on spending the rest of my life enriching the lives of others through theatre, education, and leadership. And I'm a really nice guy too!
